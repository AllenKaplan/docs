# Your Avo Workspace

The Avo workspace is where you define the data for your application. It contains
detailed definitions of your tracking plan that will be used to generate
code from and validate your data against.

The following sections build up your Avo workspace:

- <Link to="/workspace/tracking-plan">Tracking Plan</Link>: where all actions, properties and metrics are defined
- <Link to="/workspace/integration">Connections</Link>: configuring programming languages and analytics platforms
- <Link to="/workspace/implement">Implement</Link>: source specific guide to implementing Avo
- <Link to="/workspace/integrations">Integrations (beta)</Link>: sync your tracking plan with the one in your analytics platform
- <Link to="/workspace/health">Health (beta)</Link>: monitor the health of your data
